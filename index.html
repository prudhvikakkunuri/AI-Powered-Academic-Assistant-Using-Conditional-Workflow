One classifier, three case files, one desk that answers

A single LLM call sorts the incoming message into academic, fee, or general. LangGraph's conditional edges send state down exactly one of three paths — no query ever touches a retriever it doesn't need.

Case files

What each route actually does

Academic

Handbook lookup

Attendance, exams, grading, credits, promotion rules, summer training, degree requirements.

  • Retriever over academics_handbook.pdf
  • Top 4 chunks by similarity
Fee

Fee structure lookup

Tuition, late charges, refunds, scholarships — anything money-related.

  • Retriever over fee_structure.pdf
  • Top 4 chunks by similarity
General

Open conversation

Greetings and anything outside college rules or fees — answered from the model's own knowledge.

  • No retrieval step
  • Skips straight to drafting
